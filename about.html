Our Mission

To work towards a sustainable future by offering solutions for inherent plastic pollution challenges primarily in Africa, where indiscriminate disposal sends unprecedented volumes of plastic to landfills and oceans.

We're committed to developing a circular economy of plastics through participation across the entire value chain, shifting from "take-make-dispose" to a system that's circular by design.

Circular Economy

Addressing plastic waste in the environment and developing a circular economy of plastics requires participation across the value chain. We amplify circular economy solutions that shift the current "take–make–dispose" plastics system towards one that is circular by design.

A circular economy is restorative and regenerative by design. Materials constantly flow around a 'closed loop' system, retaining value indefinitely.

The value of plastic is not lost by being thrown away — it's transformed into new resources.
