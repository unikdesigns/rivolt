OUR VISION

Integrated Sustainability

Rivolt recognizes that in order to succeed in today's global marketplace, sustainability must be embedded in the way we do business.

Our vision is to integrate the environmental, social, and economic dimensions of sustainability into our core business strategy, and to ensure that throughout our business we have a deep understanding of the megatrends that will affect our company and society at large.

OUR PILLARS

The Three Dimensions

Environmental

Addressing plastic waste and protecting marine ecosystems through innovative recycling solutions.

Social

Building inclusive communities and ensuring benefits reach host communities and stakeholders.

Economic

Creating value while driving circular economy principles through sustainable business practices.

A Continuous Journey

We describe sustainability as a journey for our business that requires continuous learning, adaptation, and improvement of our basic business processes.

  • Continuous learning & adaptation
  • Regular reexamination of achievements
  • Optimization of our impact
