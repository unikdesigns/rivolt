Rivolt Recycling is a PET plastic processing company, operating within recycling, waste management and environmental remediation sectors in Africa and Internationally.

OUR DRIVE

Mission

To work towards a sustainable future by offering solutions for inherent plastic pollution challenges primarily in Africa, where indiscriminate disposal sends unprecedented volumes of plastic to landfills and oceans.

We're committed to developing a circular economy of plastics through participation across the entire value chain.
