OUR STRATEGY

Sustainable Growth
Through Innovation

In a dynamic macro-environment, we constantly review our operational model to identify growth opportunities that correlate with the demands of existing and potential clients.

Our strategy is to achieve long-term sustainable growth as a recycling solutions company by adding value to client operations with high quality products and services. We strive to be true partners with our clients, helping them achieve greater success by providing effective solutions.

Strategic Objectives

Our strategic objectives comprise four major pillars

Safety & Service

Maintain a balance between service delivery and safety across all operations.

Market Position

Grow and maintain a formidable position based on differentiated know-how and technical capabilities.

Client Relationships

Develop long-term relationships with clients through performance-based contracts.

Regional Expansion

Extend our scope of services and setup regional subsidiaries across the continent.
